Upcycled jeans: Denim Tote Bag

This tote bag was made with old jeans. The materials needed are listed below:

  1. Old jeans (I used 4 different pairs) or denim fabric
  2. Mid-weight fusible interfacing
  3. Cotton woven fabric (for the inner lining)
  4. Denim thread
  5. 30cm-long zipper
